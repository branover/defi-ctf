# Your First Flip

NFTs are just tokens with pictures — and where there are tokens, there are trades. In this challenge you'll use the **NFT Marketplace** tab to buy a corgi NFT from the floor and flip it to a buyer bot for a profit.

## Objective

Earn **0.4 ETH profit** through NFT sales (you start with 2 ETH — grow it to 2.4 ETH net).

## How to Play

1. Start the challenge.
2. Open the **NFT** tab (top navigation bar) — it appears once the chain is running.
3. Browse the marketplace listings. NFTs are priced at 0.3 WETH each.
4. A buyer bot is watching the marketplace and will pay a markup (~40%) for any NFT with rarity ≥ 50.
5. Buy an NFT by clicking **Buy** in the marketplace UI, or run the script in the JavaScript IDE.
6. List it for sale at a higher price. The buyer bot will snap it up.
7. Once your **NFT sales profit** reaches 0.4 ETH (shown on the progress bar), you win.

## Hint

Open the **NFT** tab — it only appears when a challenge with an NFT marketplace is running. Each card shows the NFT's rarity score. Rarity ≥ 50 means the buyer bot is interested. List above 0.3 WETH but keep it under 0.6 WETH so the bot will actually buy.
